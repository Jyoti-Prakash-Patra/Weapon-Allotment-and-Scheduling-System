About Us

About DRDO

The Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) is the R&D wing of the Ministry of Defence, Government of India. Established in 1958, DRDO is dedicated to enhancing India's self-reliance in defense systems. It develops a wide range of defense technologies, including aeronautics, armaments, combat vehicles, electronics, instrumentation, engineering systems, missiles, advanced computing, simulation, and more.

About PXE

Proof & Experimental Establishment (PXE) is a premier DRDO lab situated in Chandipur, Odisha. PXE is responsible for field testing and evaluation of a wide range of armament stores including rockets, bombs, torpedoes, and artillery shells. It plays a crucial role in validating the performance and safety of defense products before induction into the armed forces.

Vision & Mission of DRDO

Our Vision

Empower India with cutting-edge defense technologies and systems that ensure national security and strategic superiority in all domains.

Our Mission

Design, develop, and lead production of state-of-the-art sensors, weapon systems, platforms, and allied equipment for our Armed Forces using science and technology as a key enabler.
